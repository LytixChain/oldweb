Quick confirmations, solid tech, great network

Lytix is a fast confirmation, minable, solid masternode coin. No ICO required. This coin is minable for the first 100,000 blocks at 30 LYTX per block. Switch over to Proof of Stake and begin staking after the initial mining period. Enjoy!
